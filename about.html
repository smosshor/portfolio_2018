About me

I've worn many hats; from Human Factors Specialist to UX Designer, Instructional Designer to Research Scientist and Graphic Designer to Classroom Teacher. My interdisciplinary passion for design, technology, social science research and changing the world come together in how I approach each project, considering the subtle behavorial changes that will influence how a user interacts with the brand, product or interface.

In everything I do, I seek to:

Empower others to improve their own lives

Ensure that solutions are accesssible and inclusive

Nudge users into making better decisions for themselves

Education

I graduated from Carnegie Mellon with a B.S. in Human-Computer Interaction and Decision Science, with a Minor in Learning Media.
