Processfolio



Processfolio supports teachers and students involved in project-based learning from in-class assignments to independent studies and research, by helping them communicate and collaborate throughout the process, encouraging more meaningful reflection, and making it easy to communicate with the world by generating public facing portfolio pages.

This project came out of multiple research projects on collaboration, project-based learning and the act of creating portfolios.
