UX Research

Get to know your Users

I have experience using a variety of tools to gain quantitative and qualitative insights into how people interact with your product or experience. We'll chat about what's best for you based on time, cost and the question you're trying to answer.

Heuristic Evaluation & Expert Review

Have a prototype or existing interface and looking for some quick UX feedback? A heuristic evaluation uses Neilson's Design principles to analyze your interface, and prioritize what is most important to improve.

Rapid Prototyping

Have a bunch of ideas but not quite sure what will work best? I can help you design and run a study to gather insights on what's working best.

Usability Testing

Usability Testing is super useful to gain deep insights into how your users are interacting with your product, or a prototype. I have experience designing, running and collecting data on usability testing sessions with real-live-humans in labs with 2-way mirrors, but also on the phone, in elementary school classrooms and with video software. And I'll work with you to take the messy data and turn it into concrete, actionable findings.
