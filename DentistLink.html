Dentist Link

Finding dental solutions when you're on Medicare can be difficult, especially when you're also dealing with plenty of other problems. DentistLink is a free service designed by Arcora Foundation to help underserved communities on Medicare to locate Dental providers that take their insurance, and to support providers and social support workers in supporting them throughout the process.
